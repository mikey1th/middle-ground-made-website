Is a mnemonic device for my <a href="/snippets/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="body-link">sippet</a> “man shall not live on tech alone,” which is based on a paraphrased version of  the verse, Mathew 4:4.

When this understanding is reached, LOSTMAN communicates something beyond the scope of its literal interpretation. It tells us that we’re lost because we think we’ve found our way. It reminds us that there is a beautiful world beyond the bezels of our digital devices. It shouts that regardless of how advanced our technologies become, they will always lack the essential compenets needed to sustain life.

But even more, LOSTMAN asks us to consider that if living on tech alone results in becoming lost, what then should we do if  we ever want to become found?
