In a previous note I described Silispeak as “the sort of insidious, misleading, pharisaical language that springs forth from the technocratic hotbed of Silicon Valley.” To firm up my illustration I even provided a list of words that are, distinctly Sili. The list includes: **Asynchronous. Democratization. Efficiency. Elites. Engagement. Luxe. Monetize. Optimize. Radical. Retention. Shareholder. Unprecedented. User. Workflow.**

In this note, I’ll go through the list and provide demystified definitions of each word so that we can better understand what these Agentic-AI using, Slack message sending, “hey let’s circle back to that” saying,  productivity obsessed, 9-9-6 millionaires are up to.

**Asynchronous:** To make progress on a shared goal or project at different times.\
**Democratize:** A plan to eventually monopolize access to a productive tor service.\
**Efficiency:** An obsession with removing the slow and human elements from organizational processes.\
**Elites:** Rich people who likely inherited their money from colonizers and boomers.\
**Engagement:** The amount of internet numbers a piece of corporate media receives during any given period of time.\
**Luxe:** The best indicator that what you are about to consume is of the lowest quality.\
**Monetize:** A plan to earn profit and undoubtedly ruin a product or service users have, in some fashion become attached to or dependent upon.\
**Optimize:** An obsession with making immeasurably small process-based “improvements.”\
**Radical:** The best indicator that what you are about to consume, is super mid.\
**Retention:** An obsession with ensuring consumers of corporate propaganda choose to consume more corporate propaganda.\
**Shareholder:** The people who buy stocks or in someway benefit from the financial success of an organization.\
**Unprecedented:** The best indicator that what you are about to consume has definitely happened before.\
**User:** The people who have become addicted to gamified slop-apps and whose data is harvest and sold to the highest bidder.\
**Workflow:** The way in which someone goes about doing their work, completing a task, or pursing a project.
