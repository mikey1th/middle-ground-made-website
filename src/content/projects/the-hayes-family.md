This project means a lot to me, its an icon and a few lock ups / badges for the family my wife and I are forming. The "H" is a monogram of "Hayes" our last name. The tulips represent her and I reaching out to each other to form one cohesive picture. 

Its not everyday that you have the capacity, and the chops to take on a project like this and without a doubt, making this monogram is one of my proudest accomplishments.
